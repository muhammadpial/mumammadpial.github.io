Who We Are

RIMEX is a well-known and fast growing conglomerate in the business arena in Bangladesh. This Enterprise has started its journey with trading business in 2010.

Gradually it expanded its business into various diversified sector through establishing different wings in different business Location.

The Enterprise has been operating at present with 06 different wings with different services and products. RIMEX always focus on good quality product and services. RIMEX has enlarged its business to Automobile spare parts, Electrical Accessories, LED Lighting, Agro products, Tour and Travelling service, and Shipping service. RIMEX believes in customer trustworthiness and always very conscious to fulfill customer demand.

Vision

Mission
